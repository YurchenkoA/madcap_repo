Recovery Job Wizard for Physical Machines: Backups

On the Backups page of the Recovery Job Wizard for Physical Machines:

  1.  Select one of the views:

    • Jobs & Groups: Select one or more backups in the left pane and then select a recovery point for each backup in the right pane.

    • Backup Repositories: Select one or more backups from the Backup Repositories in the left pane and then select a recovery point for each backup in the right pane.

  2. Click Next to move to the next page.
