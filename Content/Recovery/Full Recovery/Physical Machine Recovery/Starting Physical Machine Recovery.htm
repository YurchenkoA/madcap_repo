Starting Physical Machine Recovery

 

To recover a physical machine to a VMware VM, take one of the following actions:

  • Go to Dashboard, click Recover, and select Physical Full Recovery > VM recovery from backup.



    The New Recovery Job Wizard for Physical Machines opens. 

  • Navigate to Settings and do the following:

    1. Click Repositories.

    2. Hover over the repository with the backup you want to recover.

    3. Select Physical Full Recovery > VM recovery from backup.



      The New Recovery Job Wizard for Physical Machines opens.
