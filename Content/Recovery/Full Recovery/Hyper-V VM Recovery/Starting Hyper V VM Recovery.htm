Starting Hyper-V VM Recovery

To recover entire Hyper-V VMs from backups, do one of the following:

  • Start the recovery process from the Dashboard by clicking Recover and then clicking VM recovery from backup.

  • Open the New Recovery Job Wizard from the Repositories tab following the steps below:

  1.  

    1. Click Settings in the main menu of NAKIVO Backup & Replication.

    2. Go to the Repositories tab.

    3.  Hover over the Backup Repository containing the necessary backup, click Recover and then click VM recovery from backup:

The New Recovery Job Wizard for Hyper-V opens.
