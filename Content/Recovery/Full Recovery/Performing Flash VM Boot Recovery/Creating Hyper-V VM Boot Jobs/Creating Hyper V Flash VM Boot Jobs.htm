Creating Hyper-V Flash VM Boot Jobs

To create a Hyper-V Flash VM Boot job, do one of the following:

  • Open the New Flash Boot Job Wizard from the Dashboard by clicking Recover and then clicking Flash VM Boot.

       

  • Open the Flash VM Boot wizard from the Repositories tab by following the steps below:

    1. Click Settings in the main menu of the product.

    2. Go to the Repositories tab.

    3. Hover over the name of the Backup Repository, click Recover and then click Flash VM Boot.

The New Flash Boot Job Wizard opens.
