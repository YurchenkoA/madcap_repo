Creating Amazon EC2 Replication Jobs

With NAKIVO Backup & Replication, you can replicate Amazon EC2 instances by using the workflow with a broad range of options. To create a replication job, click Create on the Dashboard and then click Amazon EC2 replication job.



The New Replication Job Wizard for Amazon EC2 opens. Complete the wizard to create a replication job.
