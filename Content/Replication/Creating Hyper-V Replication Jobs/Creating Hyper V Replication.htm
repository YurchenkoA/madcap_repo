Creating Hyper-V Replication Jobs

With NAKIVO Backup & Replication, you can replicate Hyper-V VMs using the workflow with a broad range of options. To create a replication job, click Create on the Dashboard and then click Microsoft Hyper-V replication job.

The New Replication Job Wizard for Microsoft Hyper-V opens. Complete the wizard to create a replication job.
