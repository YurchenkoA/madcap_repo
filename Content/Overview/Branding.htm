Branding

Whether you plan to use NAKIVO Backup & Replication internally or provide backup/DR-as-a-Service to external customers, you may find it beneficial to align the product's look and feel with your company's brand.

NAKIVO Backup & Replication provides a simple way to customize your product's interface so that it looks like an integral part of your organization. You can customize:

  • Product: Product title and product logo.

  • Company information: Company name and website URL.

  • Contact information: Email, support email, and contact phone.

  • Look and feel: Color and opacity of the header and footer, product background, and bookmark icon.

For information on branding configuration, refer to the Branding Configuration section.
